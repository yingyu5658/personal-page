Who am I

I am a student born in 2011 and live in Tianjin China, a blogger who pursues independent thinking, a tech enthusiast especially interested in Unix, Linux and Free Software.

I like customizing my work environment for simplicity and efficiency. I usually build things with pure C, shell script, Golang and some Front-End technology. My text editor is GNU Emacs, I love high customizability and the all-in-one workflow, as well as the philosophy of GNU and free software. I just can’t live without Emacs. I write code for fun but not make a living by it, my coding skills may not be great, but my passion for it is real. In my free time, I often read books,primarily literature, listen to music, and play the bass.
