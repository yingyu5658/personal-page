### Phone and Tablet

I use an iQOO Neo7 SE that costs less than 1,000 RMB. On my phone, I basically only need to send and receive messages, make and receive calls, and use navigation. So buying this device was probably a loss. If WeChat were no longer used across China, I might be able to cut a few hundred RMB more and buy a second-hand device from seven or eight years ago.

Around the time of the pandemic, I needed to attend online classes, so I naturally ended up with a Huawei Matepad 11. Currently, I use it for entertainment, such as watching videos, listening to music, and chatting.
